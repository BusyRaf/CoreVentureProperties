Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

The short version

When you fill out the offer request form on this site, you share some personal information with us. We use that information only to respond to your inquiry and discuss the potential purchase of your property. We don't sell it, share it for marketing, or use it for anything else.

What we collect

When you submit the offer request form, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your phone number or email address
  • The address of the property you're inquiring about
  • Your preferred timeline (optional)

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive personal data through this form.

How we collect it

Form submissions are processed by Formspree (formspree.io), a third-party form handling service. When you submit the form, your information is transmitted securely to Formspree and then forwarded to Holly McNally by email. Formspree's own privacy practices are described at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.

This website does not use cookies, analytics trackers, or any other tracking technology. No information is collected passively while you browse.

How we use it

Your information is used only to:

  • Respond to your offer request within 24 hours
  • Discuss the property and answer any questions you have
  • Make a cash offer if your property fits our buying criteria

We will only contact you in connection with your specific inquiry. We do not add you to any mailing list or send promotional messages.

Who we share it with

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party. Your information is shared only with Formspree for the purpose of delivering your submission to us (see above). No other third parties receive your data.

How long we keep it

We retain your information for as long as is reasonably necessary to follow up on your inquiry. If a transaction does not proceed, your information is not retained for any further purpose. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us directly.

Your rights

You may request to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you at any time. To do so, contact Holly directly using the information below.
